Department of Computer Applications (Aided)

Established in 2014, the Department of Computer Applications is committed to providing quality education in the field of computer science. We currently offer a three-year undergraduate Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) course, equipping students with the necessary skills to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology.
